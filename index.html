Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

How do I create an account?

Click the 'Register' button in the top navigation bar. Fill in your email and create a password to get started. It's free and takes less than a minute!

Is JobFinder free for job seekers?

Yes, JobFinder is completely free for job seekers. You can search, apply for jobs, and create your profile at no cost.

How can I upload my resume?

Once you are logged in, you can access your profile dashboard where you'll find an option to upload your resume. We recommend a PDF or DOCX format.

I'm an employer. How can I post a job?

We're glad you're interested! Please click the 'Post a Job' button in the call-to-action section or contact our sales team through the 'For Employers' link in the footer to get started.
